Who is SmartAtHome.co.uk?

SmartAtHome.co.uk is just a hobby site ran by me, Mark. I'm nice, I promise. You can email me at mark@smartathome.co.uk.

Is your website secure?

Yes, the whole site is served over https, you should see a little padlock symbol in your address bar.

What cookies do you store on my computer?

  • cookieCompliancyAccepted - this stores if you have clicked 'Understood' on the cookie warning.
  • token - if you log in to Hive via this website, this stores your auth token to communicate with Hive. It does not include your password.
  • meteo_location_id - this is the ID of the nearest weather station to your address.
  • account_no - if you log in to Octopus via this website, this stores your Octopus account number.
  • apikey - if you log in to Octopus via this website, this stores your Octopus API key.
  • postcode - this stores your postcode.
  • gsp - this stores your grid supply point, which depends on your region.
  • mpan - remembers which meter point you last looked at.
  • tariff1 / tariff2 - remembers which tariffs you most recently compared.
  • custom_unit_cost - if you use a Custom tariff on the Octopus site, this is the unit cost per kWh you set.
  • custom_charge_cost - if you use a Custom tariff on the Octopus site, this is the daily standing charge you set.
  • bearer_token - if you log in to Sonoff via this website, this stores your Sonoff authorization token. It does not include your password.
  • refresh_token - if you log in to Sonoff via this website, this stores your Sonoff refresh token, so you stay logged in. It does not include your password.
  • user_apikey - if you log in to Sonoff via this website, this stores your Sonoff user key, so you can control shared devices.
  • region - this stores your Sonoff region (EU, US or CN).
  • access_token - if you log in to Smart Life via this website, this stores your Smart Life access token. It does not include your password.
  • sl_refresh_token - if you log in to Smart Life via this website, this stores your Smart Life refresh token, so you stay logged in. It does not include your password.
  • sl_expires_in - if you log in to Smart Life via this website, this stores your Smart Life token expiry time. It does not include your password.
  • hueid - the id of your Hue hub.
  • hueuser - if you authenticate your Hue hub with this site, this is the username supplied by the hub.
  • kasa_token - if you log into Kasa via this website, this stores your Kasa authentication token. It does not include your password.
